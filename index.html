How to Use

  1. Hold your phone with its screen parallel to the ground, at the highest point of the distance you would like to measure.
  2. Press the "Start" button in our app.
  3. Drop your phone. Make sure it falls in a straight line!
  4. When you recover your phone, press the "Stop" button before picking it up.
  5. Enter your credit card information here to receive special benefits - like more accurate data!
